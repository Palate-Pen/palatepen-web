Stock & Suppliers Hub v3 — Looking Ahead Locked
The page now does three jobs, not two. See your data (KPIs at the top), see your insights (attention cards in the middle, what needs sorting today), plan accordingly (Looking Ahead at the bottom, what's coming you should get ahead of). This is the architectural directive — every surface in the product needs the third job to feel sous-chef-like rather than analyst-like.

Looking Ahead is two cards in v1 of this surface. Market move (tahini drifting up across both suppliers — likely market, not supplier-specific) and next-week-heavy (six deliveries, planning advice). Same card structure as attention cards but tagged with "PLAN FOR IT" / "GET READY" rather than severity labels. Info severity (gold bar) keeps them visually distinct from forensic intelligence above.

Delivery rows now show day, not time. Thu / Fri / Sat — chefs know roughly when their suppliers come, the system isn't pretending to have minute-precision it doesn't have. Status pills carry the urgency where it matters ("Due soon", "Arrived", "Saturday").

This pattern repeats across every surface. Chef home gets a Looking Ahead. Margins gets a Looking Ahead. Recipes gets it. Inbox gets it. Notebook gets it. Each one with surface-specific forward intelligence: tonight's covers vs prep, dish drift trajectory, recipe staleness, delivery week prep, seasonal trigger reminders.
