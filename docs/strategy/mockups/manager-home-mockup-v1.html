Manager v1 — Business Operations
Manager view mirrors chef shell structure (9 tabs) but reframes data for business operations. Home shows operational KPIs (covers, food cost, outstanding invoices, waste). Prep visibility shows brigade status at a glance. Menu builder lets the manager drag dishes into today's menu (Starters / Mains / Desserts) and print. Reporting section surfaces supplier spend breakdown, waste patterns, dish margins (what's working), staffing allocation.

Every page has a print button. Print dashboard exports the current view as a PDF optimized for A4 printing. This supports the traditional manager workflow — print the daily sheet, walk the kitchen, annotate.

Cross-tabs navigate the full operational picture. Prep tab shows brigade station-by-station. Recipes tab shows all dishes with current costing. Stock & Suppliers shows cash exposure + supplier performance. Waste shows week patterns + intervention points. Margins shows which dishes are working. Reporting aggregates everything into business summaries.
