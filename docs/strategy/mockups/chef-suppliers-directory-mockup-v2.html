Suppliers Directory v2 — Payment Terms Added
Payment terms now have their own column. Previously buried inside the ordering pattern as a third line ("Net 14 days"), they now occupy a dedicated column with three pieces of information: the headline term (Net 14 / Net 30 / Cash on delivery, with the number in italic gold), a sub-line explaining what it means ("due 14 days from invoice"), and a current-balance pill showing what's actually owed. The chef can scan the column and instantly see who's on which terms and how much cash is committed to each.

Cash on delivery suppliers get a distinct gold pill. Henstings is COD — visually distinct from net-terms suppliers so the chef knows to bring cash or a card on delivery day. The pill colour mirrors the gold-bg highlight pattern used elsewhere for special-case rows.

Balance-owed pill quantifies cash flow exposure. "£1,240 owed" on Aubrey, "no balance" on Liberty Wines. Sums to "£2,822 owed across the books" in the page subtitle. This data feeds the future Payments/Cash Flow surface and the Invoices Looking Ahead payment run forecasting.

Six-column row layout (up from five): Identity / Contact / Ordering pattern / Payment terms / Recent activity / Arrow. The grid widens to accommodate but stays scannable on standard chef-shell viewports (1280-1400px).

Cross-surface: The Order Drafter launches from each supplier's detail page (not yet designed) and from Deliveries scheduled rows. Future v3 of this directory will add a "Place order" action button on each supplier row for quick access — for now the row arrow leads to the supplier detail page which carries the action.
