Cleaning Schedule Sub-Page — Design Notes
The destination Quick Action #3 goes to. Same pattern as Probe and Issue — breadcrumb, two-column layout, FSA Reference Strip and recent sign-offs in the right column, liability footer.

Four frequency tabs at the top. Today (8/12 done) · This Week (5/9) · This Month (2/7) · Quarterly & Annual (1/4). The chef sees at a glance where they stand against every cleaning rhythm in the kitchen. Today is the default tab.

Progress card under the tabs. Big number "8/12 tasks done today" with a healthy-green progress bar (64% complete) + a Looking-Ahead-style status line: "Service starts in 3hr 40min. 4 tasks remaining — kitchen is on track if pest visual happens before 16:00." Anticipatory, not just reporting.

Three task groups for today. Opening Clean Before Service (5 tasks, all done) · Mid-Day Between Services (3 tasks, 2 done) · Before Service Tonight (4 tasks, 1 done, 1 overdue). Each task row shows: tick box (green if done, urgent-red if overdue) · task name · method line in italic ("two-stage clean & disinfect with Suma Bac D10 at 1:50 dilution") · sign-off line ("✓ Done 07:48 · Sam") · frequency tag (Daily / Weekly / Per Use).

Method line is the SFBB compliance hook. Every task carries its method — what's being used, what dilution, what process. This is what an EHO actually checks: "show me your cleaning method for X." We make it part of the task definition, not buried in a separate document.

Overdue state is loud. Deep clean walk-in fridge — last done Tue 6 May, weekly task, now 9 days overdue. Urgent-red border, exclamation icon, "⚠ Overdue · Last done Tue 6 May" in urgent-red text. This is what flows to the Chef Home's Looking Ahead as a Plan-For-It tag.

Edit Schedule + Add Task buttons. Top right. Every kitchen has its own cleaning rhythm — chef can fully edit the master schedule, add bespoke tasks (e.g. weekly grease trap, monthly extractor service), set frequencies, set "last done" baselines. Default schedule when a venue onboards: full SFBB-aligned template with all 12 standard daily, 9 weekly, 7 monthly, 4 quarterly tasks pre-loaded. Customise from there.

Right column: FSA Strip + Today's Sign-Offs. FSA Reference Strip links directly to SFBB Cleaning, Food hygiene business guidance, and Food safety management. Then a Sign-Offs panel showing the last 6 completions today with timestamp, who did it, what was done. Italic gold for menu-linked references (lamb shoulder). The chef sees their team's accountability trail at a glance.

The cross-shell loop. Cleaning data flows to: (1) the Safety home's 12-week diary calendar (green/amber/red dots), (2) Looking Ahead on the Chef Home as a behavioural pattern, (3) the EHO Mode 90-day PDF export, (4) Manager Home as a multi-shift compliance score (when on multi-site tier).
