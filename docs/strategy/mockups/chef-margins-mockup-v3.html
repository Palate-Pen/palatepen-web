Margins v3 — Mode-Shifted Voice
Serious where it matters: the two flagged attention cards (Urgent + Watch) stay in direct, kitchen-floor problem-solving voice. "Your lamb shawarma's hurting you. Aubrey hit you with £14.20/kg on lamb shoulder Tuesday." No softening, no jokes.

Dry warmth where things are holding: healthy section summaries get personality. "all behaving", "steady as ever", "pulling weight", "sweet end to the menu". The Grill section subtitle: "everyone behaving". The Sides: "making good money, all six".

Funny lives in the safe places: exposure column on holding dishes — "Chicken doing what chicken does", "Best margin on the menu" on pickled vegetables, "same story as the hummus" on the baba ghanoush soft drift. Banter exists, but never above an Urgent card or in a Watch state.

Rule for the design system: voice tracks severity of the content beneath it. Urgent/Watch = serious chef voice. Healthy/steady = dry warmth allowed. The page's most severe content sets the dominant register; warmth lives in unflagged details, never above flagged ones.
